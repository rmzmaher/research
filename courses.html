4. Teaching Philosophy

Courses are designed around a three-layer approach: (i) establish the domain physics so that model choices are motivated by the engineering problem; (ii) introduce methodology with explicit discussion of assumptions, identifiability, and validity threats; and (iii) close with practice on reproducible Python notebooks using open datasets (Volve, SPE-10, Teapot Dome). Assessment is project-based: students deliver a short methodological report with falsifiable success criteria, mirroring how research papers are reviewed.
