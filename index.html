Research Profile

Interdisciplinary researcher advancing the theoretical and practical foundations of machine learning for complex physical systems in the energy sector. Research agenda integrates physics-informed neural networks, multi-agent reinforcement learning, and hybrid modelling to address fundamental challenges in subsurface energy characterisation, predictive maintenance, and autonomous production optimisation. Doctoral work at TU Clausthal established a generalizable data analytics framework for subsurface energy systems, demonstrated across predictive maintenance, virtual flow metering, and production optimisation domains through industrial validation with ExxonMobil and Cairn Energy.

Scholarly contributions documented in 5 peer-reviewed journal articles (ACS Omega, Neural Computing & Applications, SPE Production & Operations, Franklin Open) with 232 citations (h-index: 7, i10-index: 6). Active practitioner at Weatherford contributing to product roadmaps for StrataCore, PetroVisor, Centro, and WellFlo platforms, with 9+ years of AI/ML implementation experience in oil & gas operations.
